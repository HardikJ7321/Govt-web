Together, we can stop COVID-19

Take steps to care for yourself and help protect others in your home and community.

  • Always wear a face-mask while going outside.
  • Wash your hands frequently with soaps or sanitizers.
  • Avoid close contacts and follow social distancing guidelines.
  • Avoid touching lips, eyes and nose as much as possible.
